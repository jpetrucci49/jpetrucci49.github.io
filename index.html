About Me

I'm a born and raised Bostonian. Grew up soaking in the marvel of the technology boom. Macintosh, Oregon Trail and 6.5" Floppy Drives. I've always had an affinity for tochnologies. The type to take apart their SEGA and Nintendo to see how they do; what they do.

I first began my journey into the world of development scripting simple client mods in the Counter-Strike console. At the time, I hardly understood that I was pouring the foudation for my understanding of programming, yet the days tick on.

A simple sample of a CS-Bhop script, notably based in C#:

bind "f1" "bunny_on"
alias "looper" "+jump;wait;-jump;wait;loop"
alias "bunny1" "alias loop looper"
alias "bunny2" "alias loop"
alias "+bunny" "looper;bunny1"
alias "-bunny" "bunny2;-jump"
alias "bunny_on" "bind space +bunny;bind f1 bunny_off"
alias "bunny_off" "bind space +jump;bind f1 bunny_on"

echo "BunnyHop Script Loaded!"
echo ""
echo "Press F1 to toggle script On/Off"
